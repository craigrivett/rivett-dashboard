Confidence as a speed limiter
💡 Idea Blog
A lack of confidence killed more dreams than a lack of competence ever did.
  • Confidence = speed limiter on your system — you have capacity for more but self-doubt caps your ability to chase it
  • Causes risk avoidance → you move slower than your competition
  • Angle: what would you do if you knew you wouldn't fail?

💡 Idea Blog
Don't marry the person you can have fun with — marry the person you can suffer with.
  • Anyone can love you when life is good

  • When everything's uncertain and the future looks terrifying — that's when you find out who's really there

How to give better compliments
💡 Idea Blog
Most compliments are lazy. Here's how to make yours land.
  • Be specific — "you look great in that shirt" beats "nice shirt"
  • Compliment the effort, not the success — praising only outcomes signals they only deserve praise when they win
  • Compliment the impact — instead of "thanks for your time", say "you make me feel so supported"
